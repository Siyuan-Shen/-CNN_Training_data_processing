We used MCD12Q1 v6.1 for Land Cover. 

Download website: https://e4ftl01.cr.usgs.gov/MOTA/MCD12C1.061/

It has three legends, Geospphere-Biosphere Programme (IGBP), University of Maryland (UMD), and Leaf Area Index (LAI) 
for landtype1, landtype2, and landtype3, respectively.

Details can be found in this link: https://lpdaac.usgs.gov/documents/101/MCD12_User_Guide_V6.pdf


MCD12C1 IGBP Legend 

0 - Water Bodies
1 - Evergreen Needleleaf Forests
2 - Evergreen Broadleaf Forests
3 - Deciduous Needleleaf Forests
4 - Deciduous Broadleaf Forests
5 - Mixed Forests
6 - Closed Shrublands
7 - Open Shrublands
8 - Woody Shrublands
9 - Savannas
10 - Grasslands
11 - Permanent Wetlands
12 - Croplands
13 - Urban and Built-up Lands
14 - Cropland/Natural Vegetation Mosaics
15 - Permanent Snow and Ice
16 - Barren
